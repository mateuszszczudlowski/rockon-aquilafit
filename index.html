What is Aquilafit

AquilaFit is an interactive workout guidance system for gyms and fitness clubs. Its combines technologies such as RFID, WiFi and QR codes along with cloud computing in an innovative and intuitive package designed to optimize users' training results. The system is easy to navigate and offers instant access to personal fitness data stored in the cloud and available 24/7 across a network of gyms throughout the country. AquilaFit offers a smarter, more balanced and better-informed workout.
